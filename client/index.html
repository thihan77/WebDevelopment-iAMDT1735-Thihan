Graphic Design

The ‘BA (Hons) Fashion Design’ degree course explores the exciting world of fashion. Animation & Visual Effects

The ‘BA (Hons) Animation & Visual Effects’ degree course explores the world of animated movies, content for games, visual effects (VFX), apply Creative practices and master the magic of bringing characters and stories to life through a range of animation techniques in 2D, 3D, CG, Stopmotion, etc.

Our Story

In 2005, two Artrepreneurs decided it was time to prove everyone who thought that Creative people cannot have successful careers, wrong. And the ‘AMDT School of Creativity’ was born Since then, AMDT School of Creativity has been relentless in inspiring Creative minds and brought relief to countless parents who were worried that their Creative child might not have a successful career in their chosen Creative disciplines.
